HOW TO MAKE PANCAKES

You will need- 1 cup of salted melted butter,3 drops of lemon mixed with 1 cup of milk, 2 eggs, a teaspoon of vanilla essence, a cup of normal or heated milk, a capful of oil, a teaspoon of baking powder, half a teaspoon of baking soda,3 cups of flour, a tablespoon of powdered sugar, 2 bowls, a whisk, a sieve and a pan.


-First put and the wet ingredients into one bowl and whisk.

-Next, seive the dry ingredients and put them into a different bowl and whisk.

-After making sure that all ingredients are well whisked, put them into one of the bowls and mix until there are no lumps.

Back to waffles

-Heat the pan for about 5 mins on sim with a capful of oil on the pan.

-Pour portions of the batter onto the pan one at a time and heat each portion for about 2 mins flipping it after one min.

Thats all! steaming hot pancakes ready to be devoured by you:>
