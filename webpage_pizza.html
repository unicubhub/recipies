HOW TO MAKE CHEESE PIZZA

-First knead yeast, flour and water together to a soft, yet non-sticky consistency, and let sit for 15 hrs in a fridge.


-Take some tomatoes and cut the top off.


-Put the tomatoes in a mixer and mix until it becomes sauce, and store.


-After 15hrs have passed take the pizza dough and roll it into the shape of a thick plate


-Apply the tomato sauce onto the base and grate quater a block of cheese onto base


-Take the uncooked pizza and put it in the oven for 30mins


YOUR PIZZA IS READY TO SERVE!


This pizza can be customized, simply prepare toppings and put them on top if you want.
