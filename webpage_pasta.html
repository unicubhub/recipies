HOW TO MAKE RED PASTA


-First boil 1 litre or 4 cups of water.


-Next put in half a packet of Penne pasta and soak it in the water for 15-30 mins.


-Next cut a capsicum, 2 tomatoes, 1 onion and half a zuchini(if you want you can put in minced chicken).


-Then take a saucepan and cook the vegetables in a tablespoon of oil.


-Then take either Barrila tomato pasta sauce or Ragu's original pasta sauce and a teaspoon of salt and pour desired amount in the saucepan and mix for 5 mins.


-Next drain your soaked pasta and put in the saucepan and mix.


-Finally your pasta is ready to be served!
